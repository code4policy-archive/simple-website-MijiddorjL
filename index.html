Programming and Data for Policymakers [DPI-691M]

This class is helping you:

  • Learn how to pick up new technical skills (in any language) on your own.
  • Learn how to ask the right questions to identify someone trying to sell you bad technology.
  • Understand the value of open source technology in Government.
  • Speak with experts and coders on the topic.

This class is helping you learn how to pick up new technical skills on your own, which is a valuable skill in today's rapidly changing technological landscape. It is also teaching you how to ask the right questions to identify someone trying to sell you bad technology, so that you can make informed decisions when it comes to purchasing or implementing technology solutions. Additionally, the class is helping you understand the value of open source technology in government, which can be a cost-effective and transparent way to implement technology solutions. Finally, the class is providing an opportunity for you to speak with experts and coders on the topic, which can help you deepen your understanding and expand your network of contacts in the tech industry.
